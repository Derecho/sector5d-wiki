Welcome to Sector 5D IRC Network.

h2. Connecting

You can connect to Sector 5D at irc.sector5d.org on port 6697 with SSL. You can find a full list of our [[Servers here|Servers]].

If you are having any issues connecting this may be due to your client trying to connect via IPv6 when you do not have a IPv6 connection, if this is the case you can try connecting to ipv4.irc.sector5d.org
