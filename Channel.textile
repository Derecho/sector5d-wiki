h1. #sector5d

This page is about the official Sector 5D IRC channel at #sector5d. You can find [[channel statistics|http://stats.sector5d.org/]] which are generated nightly [[here|http://stats.sector5d.org/]]


h2. Rules

* No flooding or spamming
* Please use a pastebin such as [[dpaste.de|http://dpaste.de/]] or [[pastie.org|http://pastie.org/]] when pasting more than 3 lines.
* No insulting others, or starting flamewars.
* No auto-rejoin after kick
* No advertising
